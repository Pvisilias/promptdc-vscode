# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone involved in PromptDC.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior include:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Providing constructive feedback

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or intimidation
- Trolling or personal attacks

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact **spromptdc@gmail.com**.
